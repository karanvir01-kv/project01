Corporate Information

About Us: At Typewriters.ca, we are more than just purveyors of vintage typewriters; we are champions of timeless craftsmanship. Established in 1950s, our passion for preserving the artistry of typewriters has led us to curate a collection that embodies the spirit of a craftsmanship.

Our Mission: Our mission is to introduce the magic of typewriters to the current generation, fostering an appreciation for the tangible and the authentic. We strive to provide a curated selection of meticulous typewriters that not only serve as functional writing tools but also stand as symbols of enduring craftsmanship.

Quality Assurance: At Typewriters.ca, quality is paramount. Our team of skilled artisans and technicians ensures that each typewriter undergoes a rigorous restoration process. From cleaning and refurbishing to testing and tuning, we are committed to delivering typewriters that meet the highest standards of functionality and aesthetics.

Customer Satisfaction: Customer satisfaction is at the core of our business. We take pride in offering personalized service and a seamless shopping experience. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a first-time buyer, our knowledgeable staff is dedicated to assisting you in finding the perfect typewriter to suit your needs.
