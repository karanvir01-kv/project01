Support services

Comprehensive Warranty: All our typewriters come with a comprehensive warranty to ensure peace of mind. We stand behind the quality of our products and offer support for any manufacturing defects.

Dedicated Customer Support: Our friendly and knowledgeable customer support team is ready to assist you with any inquiries, troubleshooting, or guidance. Contact us via phone, email, or live chat for prompt assistance.

Product Care Guides: Access detailed product care guides to help you maintain and care for your typewriter. These guides provide tips on cleaning, lubricating, and preserving the longevity of your cherished machine.

Typewriter Repair Services: In the rare event that your typewriter requires repair, take advantage of our typewriter repair services. Our skilled technicians can restore and fix issues to ensure your typewriter continues to function at its best.

Newsletter and Updates: Subscribe to our newsletter to receive regular updates, maintenance tips, and exclusive offers. Stay informed about the latest developments in the world of typewriters and benefit from special promotions.

Buyback and Trade-In Programs: Participate in our buyback and trade-in programs. Upgrade to a new model or sell your typewriter back to us for credit towards your next purchase.

Accessories: Explore our range of typewriter accessories, including replacement ribbons, keycap sets, and carrying cases. Enhance your typewriter experience with authentic accessories designed for optimal performance.
